Appetizer Recipes

1. Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 200g shrimp (peeled and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt, pepper, and lemon juice to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp, season, and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  4. Drizzle lemon juice before serving.

2. Crispy Potato Wedges

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium potatoes (cut into wedges)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Toss potato wedges with oil and seasonings.
  3. Bake for 25–30 minutes until golden and crisp.
